# Summary

The Apple Monitor II  (or Monitor //) modeled at 10% original size in 123DDesign. Prints in several pieces for painting and assembly.   

Most notable feature - the front bezel swivels vertically.  

Complete, assembled and painted pieces are available at https://www.etsy.com/shop/RetroConnector or https://www.shapeways.com/shops/pressurepen
